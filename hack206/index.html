Our Mission

Built for Coastal Andhra Pradesh

Coastal Andhra Pradesh faces recurring cyclones, floods, and natural disasters every year. Crisis AI was built as a unified command platform for citizens, rescue teams, and local authorities to coordinate and respond faster than ever before.
